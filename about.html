About Cristiana

Economist turned information scientist with a mandate to humanize technology.

I connect rigorous research with policy-ready insights. My background spans economics, behavioral science, and platform design, equipping me to interrogate how digital systems affect markets and wellbeing.

Academic foundations

I am a PhD Candidate in Information Science at Cornell University and an IvyPlus Scholar at Stanford's Department of Economics. My scholarship is grounded in industrial organization and behavioral economics.

I believe that equitable digital futures hinge on understanding where design incentives misalign with user wellbeing—and on translating that evidence into enforceable policy.
